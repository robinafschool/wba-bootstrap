Autokafka - AI Homework Website

Project Overview

Autokafka is an AI-powered website that helps students generate history homework.

Technologies Used
Python AI Integration Flask JavaScript
Key Features
  • AI-generated homework
  • YouTube transcription
  • User authentication system

Development Process

I built this project over four months, focusing on creating a reliable AI system that could generate historically accurate content. The biggest challenge was eliminating the AI's tendency to hallucinate and having it stick to only what the video talked about.

What I Learned

This project helped me understand how to effectively integrate AI services with web applications, manage user authentication securely, and optimize for performance when dealing with AI-generated content.

Bloxcode - Programming Agency

Project Overview

Bloxcode started as a programming agency where I led a team of programmers and found clients for them, taking a percentage of the revenue. Later, I pivoted to selling pre-made assets after finding team management too challenging.

Business Model Evolution
Team Management Client Acquisition Digital Products E-commerce
Key Achievements
  • Built a team of 5 programmers
  • Secured contracts with 12+ clients
  • Created a portfolio of 30+ pre-made assets
  • Established recurring revenue model

Business Challenges

Managing a remote team of programmers proved more difficult than expected. Communication issues, varying skill levels, and deadline management were significant hurdles. Pivoting to a digital product model allowed for more predictable revenue and fewer management headaches.

What I Learned

This venture taught me valuable lessons about team leadership, client relations, digital product development, and the importance of adapting business models based on practical realities rather than just initial plans.
